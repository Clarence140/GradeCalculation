Elementary Grade Calculator

Enter 6 subject grades to compute your simple average.

How to Calculate Your Grades

  1. Enter your grades for each subject in the calculator on the left.
  2. Make sure each grade is between 0 and 100.
  3. Click the Calculate Average button.
  4. The calculator will add all your grades together.
  5. It will then divide the total by the number of subjects (6).
  6. Your average grade will be shown below the calculator.
  7. If any grade is missing or invalid, you will see an error message.
  8. Use whole numbers or decimals (e.g., 89.5) for precision.
  9. Switch themes using the button at the top to match your environment.
  10. Review your lowest subjects and plan improvements for next term.
  11. Save or screenshot your result to track your progress over time.
